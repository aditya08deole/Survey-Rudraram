# Rudraram Survey - User Guide

## Getting Started
1. **Landing Page**: The entrance providing a high-level overview.
2. **Onboarding**: An interactive tour will guide you on your first dashboard visit.

## Core Features
### 1. Unified Map
- **Markers**: Devices are color-coded by type.
- **Clustering**: High-density areas are automatically grouped for clarity.
- **Search & Filter**: Use the header controls to narrow down by zone or status.

### 2. Analytics (Insights)
- **Trends**: View 14-day data ingestion progress.
- **Health Scores**: Monitor infrastructure health by zone (Working vs Not Working).
- **Distribution**: Visualize your asset mixture (Borewells, Sumps, Tanks).

### 3. Data Center (Table View)
- **High Performance**: Virtualized scrolling for massive datasets.
- **Filtering**: Live search and multi-select filters.
- **Map Sync**: Click "View on Map" to instantly jump to a device's location.

### 4. Media Management
- **Galleries**: Click any device to see its photo gallery in the sidebar.
- **Uploads**: Add new photos directly from the field with automatic compression.

### 5. Team & Sync
- **Sync Status**: Real-time view of the Excel-to-Supabase pipeline health.
- **Team**: Standardized role management (Admin/Editor/Viewer).

## Offline Usage
This app is a Progressive Web App (PWA). If you use it in the field:
- **Map Caching**: Most map tiles are cached once viewed.
- **Connectivity Banner**: An "Offline" banner appears when the internet is lost.
- **Read-only Mode**: Data fetching will serve from the local cache when offline.
